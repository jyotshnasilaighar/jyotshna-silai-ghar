# 📄 **Business Requirement Specification (BRS)**

## Project: **Jyotshna Silai Ghar Website**

---

## 1. 📌 **Project Overview**

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a responsive, user-friendly, and visually appealing website for **Jyotshna Silai Ghar**, a stitching and tailoring brand.

The website will:

* Showcase stitching tutorials (Kurti, Blouse, Sleeve designs)
* Promote the YouTube channel
* Generate customer leads for tailoring services
* Build a strong digital brand presence

---

## 2. 🎯 **Business Objectives**

* Increase YouTube subscribers and video engagement
* Build a recognizable online brand
* Attract customers for stitching services
* Provide educational content (tutorials)
* Establish trust and credibility

---

## 3. 👥 **Target Audience**

* Women interested in stitching and tailoring
* Beginners learning sewing
* Local customers for custom stitching services
* Odia-speaking audience (Primary)
* Hindi & English users (Secondary)

---

## 4. 🌐 **Project Scope**

### ✅ In Scope:

* Website design & development
* Mobile-responsive layout
* SEO optimization
* YouTube integration
* Contact & inquiry system

### ❌ Out of Scope:

* Payment gateway (Phase 1)
* User login system (Note: Basic Auth implemented for order tracking)
* Advanced analytics dashboard

---

## 5. 🧩 **Functional Requirements**

### 5.1 🏠 Home Page

* Hero banner with tagline
* Intro about Jyotshna Silai Ghar
* Highlighted services
* Featured YouTube videos
* Call-to-action buttons (Subscribe / Contact)

---

### 5.2 📖 About Us Page

* Business story
* Experience and expertise
* Vision & mission

---

### 5.3 🧵 Services Page

Services to include:

* Kurti Stitching
* Blouse Stitching
* Sleeve Designs
* Custom Tailoring

Each service should include:

* Description
* Images
* Optional pricing

---

### 5.4 🖼️ Gallery Page

* Image showcase of designs
* Categories:

  * Kurti
  * Blouse
  * Sleeves

---

### 5.5 🎥 Video Section

* Embedded YouTube videos
* “Watch on YouTube” button
* Playlist integration

---

### 5.6 📞 Contact Page

* Contact form:

  * Name
  * Phone
  * Message
* WhatsApp integration
* Location/map (optional)

---

## 6. 🎨 **Non-Functional Requirements**

* Fully responsive (mobile + desktop)
* Fast loading speed
* Simple and clean UI/UX
* SEO optimized
* Secure and reliable

---

## 7. 🎨 **UI/UX Requirements (Simple + Premium)**

### 🔥 Premium Version:

* Modern UI (bold typography + visuals)
* High-quality images
* Smooth scrolling effects
* CTA buttons with highlights
* Section-based storytelling design

---

## 8. 🔍 **SEO Requirements**

* Target keywords: Stitching tutorials, Kurti stitching, Blouse stitching, Tailoring Odia
* Meta title & description
* Image ALT tags
* SEO-friendly URLs

---

## 9. 🛠️ **Technology Stack**

* Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS, Motion
* Backend: Firebase (Auth, Firestore)
* Integration: YouTube, WhatsApp

---

## 10. 📅 **Project Timeline**

| Phase                | Duration |
| -------------------- | -------- |
| Requirement & Design | 3–5 days |
| Development          | 5–7 days |
| Testing              | 2–3 days |

---

## 11. 📈 **Success Metrics**

* Increase in YouTube subscribers
* Website traffic growth
* Number of inquiries/leads
* Engagement rate
